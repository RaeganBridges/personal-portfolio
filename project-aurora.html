Aurora Festival

Identity, motion toolkit · Client: Aurora Arts Nonprofit · 2025

Aurora Festival needed a flexible identity that could stretch from intimate gallery openings to large-scale outdoor projections. The mark references northern lights through simplified ribbon forms, while the type pairs a sturdy grotesk with a sharp display cut for headlines.

Deliverables

Logo system with clear-space rules, duotone and full-color palettes, motion stingers for social, wayfinding icons, and a 24-page sponsor deck template built in Figma for the internal team.

Process

Workshops with the board clarified three pillars: accessibility, experimentation, and local craft. Sketches moved quickly into vector grids; motion tests in After Effects informed how gradients should behave in static layouts so both felt cohesive.
