Field & Co.

Pantry and provisions rebrand · Packaging suite · 2024

Field & Co. shifted from farmers-market stalls to regional retail. The identity needed to feel rooted and tactile without looking rustic in a clichéd way. Illustration leans on botanical line work with a single warm earth tone anchoring the range.

Packaging

Labels use a repeatable pattern band and clear product hierarchy: line, flavor, net weight. Dielines were coordinated with the co-packer; barcodes and nutrition blocks sit in a dedicated quiet zone for compliance.

Rollout

Launch included in-store posters, a simple one-page brand guidelines PDF for buyers, and social templates so the small team could stay on-grid during seasonal drops.
