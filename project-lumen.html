Lumen Press

Independent poetry publisher · Series design · 2024

Lumen Press publishes limited-run poetry chapbooks. The brief was to unify five distinct voices under one spine and cover system while giving each title its own cover art language derived from a shared modular grid.

Design system

A narrow column grid, generous margins, and a single accent ink per season. Interior spreads use a restrained hierarchy: large titles, quiet folios, and footnotes set in a smaller optical size for comfortable long reading.

Production

Covers printed two-color offset on textured stock; belly bands carry ISBN and colophon. Files were prepared with print-specific black builds and folding mockups verified with the binder before final plates.
