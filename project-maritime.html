Maritime Museum

Summer exhibition campaign · Out-of-home & print · 2023

The museum’s special exhibition traced migration stories along coastal trade routes. Campaign art paired archival map textures with contemporary photography, cropped tight for readability on transit panels and wild postings.

Campaign toolkit

Key art lockups in vertical, horizontal, and square ratios; a headline hierarchy that works in three languages; and color variants for daylight vs. backlit media.

Impact

The series ran across the waterfront district for eight weeks. Ticket pre-sales exceeded the prior year’s summer show; the in-house team reused the grid for wayfinding inside the gallery.
