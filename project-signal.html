Signal Labs

B2B analytics product · UI art direction · 2023

Signal Labs asked for a visual layer that could make dense dashboards feel legible and calm. Art direction focused on contrast, spacing, and a limited set of chart accents so engineering could implement consistently in their component library.

UI principles

Eight-point spacing rhythm, semantic color tokens for success and alert states, and illustration spots for empty states that reinforce the “signal vs. noise” narrative without cluttering functional screens.

Handoff

Redlined Figma files, annotated exports, and a short motion spec for loading and success micro-interactions. Close collaboration with the lead front-end developer kept border radii and elevation aligned with platform defaults.
